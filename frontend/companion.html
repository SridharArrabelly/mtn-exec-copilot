Bring Nuru into this call

Paste the meeting join link, then open the joiner. It opens in a separate window (kept outside the Teams meeting window on purpose, to avoid audio feedback). Keep that window open for the duration of the call.

How to ask her things

Once Nuru shows as in the call, anyone can unmute and say the wake phrase (e.g. "Hey Nuru") followed by a question. She answers aloud, grounded in the knowledge base, and stays quiet otherwise so she never talks over people.

If she doesn't join, or drops out
  • Confirm the joiner window is still open and shows "Nuru is in the call".
  • If she's stuck in the lobby, admit her from the meeting roster.
  • Re-open the joiner with the button above to rejoin.
  • If the panel says Phase 2b isn't enabled, the deployment needs ACS configured (see the team's deployment notes).
