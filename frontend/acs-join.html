🎙️ Bring Nuru into a Teams meeting

This page joins a Microsoft Teams meeting as an audio assistant using Azure Communication Services. Once joined, anyone in the call can say a wake phrase (e.g. "Hey Nuru") and ask a question aloud; Nuru answers by voice, grounded in the knowledge base.

Please notify participants: an AI assistant is present and listening in the meeting. Nuru does not record or transcribe the meeting — she only listens live to answer when addressed.
